# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in the Sunsteer community to be a respectful, harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. As contributors and
maintainers, we pledge to keep our interactions open, welcoming, and professional.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that helps build a positive community:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints, experiences, and skill levels.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.
- Showing empathy and patience toward other community members.

Harassment, personal attacks, and other unprofessional or disrespectful conduct are not
tolerated. This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant], version 2.1**, as its full
code of conduct; please read it for the complete description of expected behaviour,
unacceptable behaviour, and enforcement guidelines.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull
requests, and discussions — and when an individual is representing the project in public
spaces.

## Reporting and enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it privately to the
maintainer at **`mvelten773@gmail.com`**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly, and the maintainer will respect the privacy and security of the
reporter.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may take
appropriate, fair corrective action — including editing or removing contributions, or
temporarily or permanently restricting participation — in response to behaviour they deem
inappropriate.
